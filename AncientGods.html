Ancient Gods' epic battle


During the Skypiea arc, the Gods of Rain, Earth, and the Forest were spoken of in prayer alongside the Sun God, and their existence was confirmed again in Chapter 1138 with the revelation of the Harley Texts and the Elbaf mural.


The Harley Texts, translated by Nico Robin, read: "Flames engulfed the land, and humans succumbed to their desires, touching the forbidden Sun. The enslaved prayed, and the Sun God appeared. The Earth God was enraged, and together with the serpent of infernal flames, shrouded the world in death and darkness."


According to the Harley Texts, the world of One Piece has already been destroyed twice in the past, and both times the Sun God Nika's name is involved. The Earth God and the Serpent of Infernal Flames joined hands and fought the Sun God. In the Second World: the Forest God sent forth demons, the Sun spread the fires of war, humans slew the Sun and became Gods, and the Sea God raged.


Nika — the Sun God — is a mythical warrior from ancient times, and the legend is preserved within the nearly extinct Buccaneer race. Modern documents have largely erased his legend.


The Ancient Weapons Poseidon, Pluton, and Uranus are each named after Greek deities affiliated with the sea, earth, and sky. Before Shirahoshi, Poseidon in the Void Century was also a mermaid princess who made a pact with Joyboy.
